Explain what you could observe, the differences, if AVIF is a good replacement for WebP, etc.

AVIF presents excellent compression efficiency and image quality, outperforming both JPEG and WebP in terms of smaller file sizes without compromising visual fidelity. However, its adoption might be hindered by its limited compatibility across various browsers and platforms. While major browsers like Chrome and Firefox have begun to support AVIF, older browsers and certain platforms may not fully support it yet. Therefore, when choosing between image formats, it's crucial to consider compatibility requirements, especially if broader support across different browsers and platforms is essential for your application or website. Despite its superior compression capabilities, the decision to use AVIF over more widely supported formats like WebP should be weighed against the potential limitations in accessibility across different user environments.
